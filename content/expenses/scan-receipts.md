## Steps

1. Tap the **Expenses** tab → **+**.
2. Choose **Scan Receipt** to open the document scanner.
3. Hold your phone over the receipt — the scanner highlights the document outline automatically.
4. Tap the shutter (or let the scanner capture automatically when it's happy with the frame).
5. Review the scanned image; tap **Retake** if needed, then confirm.
6. Yoley reads the receipt on-device and fills in the **Amount**, **VAT**, **Supplier** and **Date** fields for you.
7. Check the pre-filled details, adjust any field if needed, pick a category, then tap **Save**.

> **Tip:** Scanning works entirely on your device — no internet connection is required and your receipt images are never sent to a server.

> **Note:** If the scanner can't confidently detect a total from a label (e.g. "Total" or "Grand Total"), it falls back to the largest monetary figure on the receipt. Always double-check the **Amount** field before saving.
