| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| `status:draft` Draft | Saved but not yet sent to the customer. |
| `status:sent` Sent | Delivered to the customer via email or SMS. |
| `status:opened` Opened | Customer has opened the quote link. |
| `status:accepted` Accepted | Customer has accepted — ready to schedule and invoice. |
| `status:declined` Declined | Customer declined the quote. |
| `status:expired` Expired | Past the validity date without response. |
