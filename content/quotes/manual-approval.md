When a customer approves over the phone, via text or by any other method not captured in the app, you can record the approval manually.

1. Open the quote from the Quotes list.
2. Tap the green **Manually Approve** button next to the status badge at the top of the screen.
3. Select how the customer approved: **Phone**, **Text**, **Email**, or **Other**.
4. Optionally add a comment (e.g. "Agreed to proceed, start date 15th").
5. Tap **Confirm Approval**.

> **Tip:** The comment you add during manual approval is automatically carried through as a note on the job when you schedule it.
