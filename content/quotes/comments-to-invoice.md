Comments and notes on a quote flow through two places:

- **Into the job** — when you schedule a job from an accepted quote, the approval comment is pre-filled as the job note.
- **Into the invoice** — when you convert an accepted quote to an invoice, the quote's notes copy across so the customer sees the same information.

You can always edit or remove them before sending.
