Because Yoley encrypts your data locally and syncs it to **your** iCloud account, a lost or stolen phone doesn't put your data at risk.

## To restore on a new phone

1. Sign in to your new iPhone with the same Apple ID.
2. Install Yoley from the App Store.
3. Open Yoley — it'll pull your data down from iCloud automatically.

## A small note on sync timing

Yoley syncs to iCloud roughly every 5 minutes. So if you worked on something in the minutes just before losing the phone, a small amount of the very latest work could be missing when you restore.

> **Tip:** If your phone is stolen rather than lost, also use **Find My** on another Apple device to lock or erase it remotely. That doesn't affect your iCloud backup — your Yoley data will still restore onto your replacement handset.
