Yoley stores your data locally on your device in an encrypted form, and syncs it to **your** iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit. That means:

- Your customer records, quotes, invoices and receipts never sit on a Yoley server.
- Backups are encrypted and scoped to your Apple ID, just like your photos and Notes.
- Only you (and people you explicitly share an account or data export with) can access it.

> **Tip:** Turn on Face ID or a PIN in **Settings → Security** for an extra layer of protection if your phone gets left unlocked.
