How the calculation works

Your day rate isn’t a guess — it comes from your own numbers. We add the income you want to your business costs to get the total you need to bill in a year, then divide by your billable days. That gives your minimum day rate. The suggested rate adds your profit buffer on top so a couple of overruns or a quiet fortnight don’t put you in the red.
