🚀 Getting Started

Yoley is a free iOS app for UK tradespeople. Download it from the App Store and you’re ready in under two minutes — no forms, no sales calls, no credit card required.

Download & Install

  1. 1Open the App Store on your iPhone and search for Yoley.
  2. 2Tap Get (it’s free). The app installs in seconds.
  3. 3Open Yoley and follow the brief onboarding — enter your name, business name, and pick your trade.
  4. 4That’s it. You’re in.
📱 Yoley currently requires iPhone running iOS 16 or later. iPad support is coming soon.

Setting Up Your Business Profile

Tap the burger menu (three lines, top left on any screen) and select Settings. From here you can set:

  • ●
    Business name — appears on all quotes and invoices.
  • ●
    Business address — shown on quote footers for HMRC compliance.
  • ●
    Logo — upload your company logo to brand your quotes and invoices.
  • ●
    VAT number — if you’re VAT registered, add your number here and it appears on all documents.
  • ●
    Payment terms — set your default payment due date (e.g. 14 or 30 days).

KPI Cards

The four KPI tiles at the top show:

The lower section shows any jobs scheduled for today. Tap a job to open the full job detail, including map and commute time.

📝 Quotes

Create professional, branded quotes in seconds. Yoley tracks each quote through its full lifecycle from draft to accepted, and handles customer approval — even when it happens over the phone.

Creating a Quote

  1. 1Tap the Quotes tab → + (top right).
  2. 2Search for an existing customer or tap New Customer to add one on the fly.
  3. 3Enter a job title (e.g. “Full bathroom refit”). Add a reference number if needed.
  4. 4Tap Add Line Item to add labour, materials, or use a Kit Bundle.
  5. 5Optionally add notes, photos or a validity expiry date.
  6. 6Tap Save to keep as a draft, or Send to deliver immediately.
💡 Kit Bundles let you save common job packages (e.g. “Standard boiler service”) and add them with a single tap. Set up bundles in Settings → Kit Bundles.

Quote Statuses

Manually Approving a Quote

When a customer approves over the phone, via text or by any other method not captured in the app, you can record the approval manually:

  1. 1Open the quote from the Quotes list.
  2. 2Tap the green Manually Approve button next to the status badge at the top of the screen.
  3. 3Select how the customer approved: Phone, Text, Email, or Other.
  4. 4Optionally add a comment (e.g. “Agreed to proceed, start date 15th”).
  5. 5Tap Confirm Approval.
💡 The comment you add during manual approval is automatically carried through as a note on the job when you schedule it.

Sending a Quote

From any quote, tap Send. Choose to deliver by:

  • ✉Email — sends a formatted email with a link to the branded quote page.
  • 💬SMS — sends a short message with the quote link.
  • 🔗Share Link — copies the link so you can share via WhatsApp or any other channel.

💰 Invoices

Invoices in Yoley include a payment link by default so customers can pay online as soon as they open the invoice — no chasing required.

Converting a Quote to Invoice

  1. 1Open an Accepted quote.
  2. 2Tap Convert to Invoice at the bottom of the screen.
  3. 3All line items, customer details and notes copy across automatically.
  4. 4Set a due date (your default payment terms apply, but you can override).
  5. 5Tap Send Invoice.

Creating a Standalone Invoice

You don’t need a quote first. Tap Invoices → + and fill in the customer and line items directly.

Payment Links

Every invoice includes a secure payment link. When your customer taps the link, they can pay by card at 1.49% (Free plan) or 0.99% (Pro). Funds settle within 1–2 working days.

🔒 Payments are processed by Monek, a UK-regulated payment provider. Card data never touches Yoley’s systems.

📷 Expenses

Track every business expense. Snap a receipt and Yoley’s on-device OCR extracts the amount, VAT and supplier — then files it under the correct HMRC SA103 box automatically.

Scanning a Receipt

  1. 1Tap Expenses → Scan Receipt (camera icon).
  2. 2Hold your phone over the receipt. OCR runs on-device and extracts the total, VAT amount and supplier name.
  3. 3Review the extracted values — you can edit any field if the scan missed something.
  4. 4Choose an HMRC category from the list.
  5. 5Tap Save.
📷 All OCR processing happens on your device. Your receipt images are not uploaded anywhere.

📅 Job Diary

The Job Diary shows a 14-day rolling calendar of your scheduled work. Schedule jobs from accepted quotes or create standalone appointments. Each job shows the customer location on a map with live commute time from where you are.

Scheduling a Job

  1. 1Tap Job Diary → Schedule Job (top right).
  2. 2The sheet shows three options: accepted quotes ready to schedule, unaccepted quotes you can approve on the spot, and New Job (No Quote).
  3. 3Select a quote or tap New Job.
  4. 4Set the start time and end time. Add or edit any job notes.
  5. 5Tap Save Job.

Approving a Quote from the Job Diary

If a quote hasn’t been accepted yet but the customer has verbally confirmed, tap it in the schedule sheet and choose Approve & Schedule. Select the approval method (Phone, Text, Email, Other) and add a comment before continuing to the schedule form.

Job Detail & Map

Tap any job in the diary to open its full detail view. This includes:

  • 📍An interactive map showing the job’s address (from the customer record).
  • 🚘Driving commute time from your current location, calculated via Apple Maps.
  • 🔗An Open in Apple Maps button for turn-by-turn directions.
  • 🗒Job notes, status, linked quote and customer details.

Weather Forecast

⭐ Pro feature — A 5-day weather forecast is shown at the top of the Job Diary. Each day shows the icon, high and low temperature. Useful for planning outdoor work in advance.

👥 Customers

Yoley’s CRM keeps every customer’s contact details, address, job history, quotes and invoices in one place.

Adding a Customer

  1. 1Tap Customers → +.
  2. 2Enter first name, last name, phone number and email address.
  3. 3Add the customer’s address (used for job location maps and quote footers).
  4. 4Tap Save.

Customer History

Opening a customer record shows the full history: all quotes sent, all invoices raised, all jobs completed, and total revenue from that customer.

Setting Up Card Payments

First-time setup requires identity verification (takes 2–3 minutes). Go to Settings → Card Payments and follow the prompts. Once verified, payment links and Tap to Pay are active immediately.

🏦 CIS — Construction Industry Scheme

Yoley supports CIS subcontractor management for contractors who engage other tradespeople on site. Track UTR numbers, deduction rates and payment records.

Adding a CIS Subcontractor

  1. 1Tap Customers and find or add the subcontractor.
  2. 2Toggle CIS Subcontractor on.
  3. 3Enter their UTR number and select the deduction rate (20% standard, 30% unverified, or 0% gross payment status).
  4. 4Save. CIS deduction is calculated automatically on any invoice raised against them.
⚠️ Yoley helps you track CIS but does not submit returns to HMRC on your behalf. Consult an accountant for CIS submission.

📲 Tap to Pay on iPhone

⭐ Pro feature

Turn your iPhone into a card machine. Accept contactless Visa, Mastercard and Apple Pay at 0.99% — no card reader, no additional hardware.

Taking a Payment on Site

  1. 1Open the invoice → tap Collect Payment → Tap to Pay.
  2. 2Enter the amount (defaults to the invoice total).
  3. 3Present your iPhone to the customer. They tap their card, phone or watch.
  4. 4Payment confirmed. Invoice automatically marks as Paid.
📱 Tap to Pay requires iPhone XS or later running iOS 16+. No account or card reader is required beyond a Yoley Pro subscription.

🏦 Open Banking

⭐ Pro feature

Connect your business bank account to Yoley. Incoming payments are automatically matched to outstanding invoices so your books stay up to date without manual entry.

Connecting Your Bank

  1. 1Go to Settings → Open Banking.
  2. 2Find your bank from the list (supports all major UK banks).
  3. 3Authorise read-only access via your bank’s own app or website. Yoley never sees or stores your banking credentials.
  4. 4Transactions sync automatically. Yoley highlights any that match open invoices.

💬 WhatsApp & Social Enquiries

⭐ Pro feature

Capture new job enquiries from WhatsApp, Facebook, Instagram and Google directly into your Yoley pipeline. Never lose a lead in a DM again.

Once configured, enquiries from connected channels appear as leads in the Pipeline view. Tap a lead to review the message, create a customer record and convert it into a quote — all without leaving Yoley.

💰 Yoley Capital

⭐ Pro feature

Yoley Capital provides short-term cash advances to help you bridge the gap between starting a big job and getting paid. Advances are based on your invoicing history within Yoley.

To see if you’re eligible, go to Settings → Yoley Capital. Eligibility and available amounts update as your transaction history grows.

🔒 Security

Yoley uses Apple’s built-in security features to keep your business data private on your device.

Face ID / Touch ID App Lock

  1. 1Tap the burger menu (top left) → scroll down to App Lock.
  2. 2Toggle App Lock on.
  3. 3Next time you open Yoley, you’ll be asked to authenticate with Face ID or Touch ID before the app opens.
🔒 If biometrics fail (e.g. your face isn’t recognised), the app falls back to your iPhone passcode.

CloudKit Sync & Backup

All your data is automatically backed up and synced via Apple CloudKit. Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account — Yoley’s servers never hold your business data. If you get a new iPhone, sign in with the same Apple ID and everything restores automatically.
