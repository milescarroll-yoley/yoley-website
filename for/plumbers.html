Plumbing is a high-emergency, high-admin trade. One day you’re doing a £8,000 boiler swap with a Worcester Bosch quote, a Gas Safe certificate and a warranty registration to deal with. The next you’re out at 2am for a burst pipe with a handwritten ticket and a Screwfix receipt. Yoley is built so both feel the same to your admin: fast, tidy, in one app.

With Yoley you can send a branded quote for a combi swap in under two minutes using Kit Bundles, raise the invoice the moment the job’s signed off, and take payment by card on the spot with Tap to Pay at 0.99% — no card reader. Expenses from Plumbase, City Plumbing, Wolseley and Screwfix snap in via the camera and map themselves to HMRC SA103 categories for your self-assessment.

Whether you do boiler installs, central heating upgrades, bathroom fits, emergency call-outs, landlord gas safety certs or work as a CIS subcontractor on new-build, Yoley handles the paper side so you can stay under the sink.

🛠 A typical day

Morning boiler quote → evening payment

1

09:00 — Boiler survey in Kingston

You check the existing boiler location, flue run, gas supply. In the van you tap Quotes → + → "Mr Patel" → add the "Worcester 30Si swap + system clean" Kit Bundle. Tweak the flue length line, tap Send. Quote is on his email before you pull out of the drive.

2

11:30 — Emergency call-out

Tenant’s hot water packed up. You pop into Plumbase for a pump. Scan the £94 receipt at the counter — supplier and VAT captured automatically against today’s date.

3

15:00 — Invoice the emergency job

Back on site you fix the pump, test, tidy up. Invoice → + → £240 labour + £94 parts. Tap Send. The customer’s email has a "Pay now" button, or you tap her card straight on your iPhone with Tap to Pay. Either way, you’re paid before you leave.

4

19:00 — Mr Patel accepts the boiler quote

Push notification: Quote Accepted. You schedule the install for next week in the diary and move on.
