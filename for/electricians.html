Most electricians we talk to are juggling three apps, a spreadsheet and a pile of paper receipts. Yoley puts quotes, invoices, expenses, job diary, customer records and card payments in one place on your iPhone — with no monthly subscription to sign up to and no "starter plan" that locks half the features behind £29/month.

Yoley is built in the UK specifically for trades. Your quotes look professional, your expenses map straight to HMRC SA103 categories, and if you take on subcontractors Yoley handles CIS deductions automatically — on the free plan, not behind a paid tier like QuickBooks. When a customer pays, they can pay by card at 1.49%, by Tap to Pay on your iPhone at 0.99%, or by Open Banking at no fee at all.

If you’re doing domestic rewires, EICRs, consumer unit upgrades, EV charger installs, commercial fit-outs or landlord certs, Yoley gives you a fast way to quote, send, get paid and stay on top of your cashflow without a laptop.

Five reasons it’s different

📝

Quotes for EICRs and rewires in 60 seconds

Save common jobs as Kit Bundles — "Standard EICR 3-bed", "Consumer unit upgrade 10-way", "EV charger install 7kW" — and add the full priced line item list with one tap.

📷

Scan wholesaler receipts instantly

Whether it’s CEF, Edmundson, Newey & Eyre or Screwfix, snap the receipt and Yoley’s on-device OCR pulls the total, VAT and supplier. No typing, no lost paper.

💳

Take payment before you leave the job

Send a payment link in the invoice email at 1.49%, or use Tap to Pay on iPhone at 0.99% to take a contactless card or Apple Pay right on site. No card reader, no hardware.

🏦

CIS handled free

If you work as a subcontractor or hire one, Yoley calculates the 20% / 30% / 0% CIS deduction automatically on the invoice. QuickBooks charges £32/month for this. Yoley doesn’t.

📅

Diary with live weather and commute

See today’s jobs with travel time from where you are now, plus the 5-day forecast so you know when to book an outside job like a CCU or an EV charger install.

🛠 A typical day with Yoley

Morning survey → evening payment

1

08:30 — You survey a rewire

You walk the property, note 3-bed, 2 circuits extra, consumer unit upgrade. In the van you tap Quotes → + → pick customer → add the "Full 3-bed rewire" Kit Bundle. Edit two lines, tap Send. Customer has it on email before you’ve started the engine.

2

10:15 — Customer accepts on their phone

Push notification in your pocket. The quote is now Accepted. You tap through, convert it to a booked job, and drag it into next Tuesday in the Job Diary.

3

14:00 — Wholesaler stop

You grab £420 of cable and consumer unit kit from CEF. At the till you snap the receipt. Yoley extracts £420.00, VAT £70.00, supplier CEF, category Materials. Done — your expenses for the job are already logged.

4

Tuesday evening — Job done, invoice sent

Rewire complete. In the van: tap the job, Convert to Invoice, check totals, tap Send. Customer gets an email with a payment link. Five minutes later they’ve paid by card. 1.49% fee, money in your bank within 1–2 working days.
