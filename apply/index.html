Consent & declaration

By submitting this form you confirm the information is accurate, and you consent to Yoley Limited sharing it with Monek Group Limited so they can assess and process your application for a merchant account. Monek will handle your data under their own privacy terms; Yoley handles it under our Privacy Policy. Yoley acts as an introducer and is an Introducer Appointed Representative arrangement with Monek; the decision to open an account rests with Monek. We also collect basic technical information about your device and connection (such as device type and IP address) for fraud prevention and security, which is shared with Monek as part of your application.

We pass your application to Monek over a secure connection. We never store your card customers' details, and your bank details are used only to set up your account.
